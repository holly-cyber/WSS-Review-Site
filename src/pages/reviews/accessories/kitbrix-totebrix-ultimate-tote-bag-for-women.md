## Comfort & Fit

The KitBrix Extra Large Tote Bag gets top marks for comfort and design. The padded shoulder strap is an absolute game-changer. It distributes weight evenly, so even when it’s loaded with gym gear, a laptop, and a spare hoodie, and it feels comfortable. I tested this bag over two days, dashing around London (tube, coffee shops, client meetings, gym you name it), and it didn’t miss a beat.

What really impressed me was how light it feels. The 600D polyester material is strong but not heavy, so even when the bag is full, it doesn’t feel like you’re lugging a brick. The reinforced handles make hand-carrying easy, and it actually _sits right_ under your arm—no slipping or awkward shoulder readjustments mid-stride.

And let’s talk interior comfort, yes, bags can have that! The soft lining protects delicate items (hello, laptop and sunglasses), and the semi-rigid structure holds its shape beautifully without poking or pressing against your side. If you’ve ever wrestled with a floppy, overstuffed tote, you’ll appreciate how balanced and stable this one feels.

## Sizing

When KitBrix says “extra large,” they’re not kidding, but it’s large in a _useful_ way, not a “where did my keys go?” kind of way. This tote easily swallowed my gym gear, trainers, towel, water bottle, snacks, and still had room for my laptop and jacket. The BaseBrix™ moulded base is genius, it keeps everything upright and stable, even when the bag’s full. The large size can start to feel heavy or cumbersome if overpacked.

Inside, there are multiple compartments that make organising a dream. I had my keys and wallet tucked away in the smaller pockets, while my shoes lived happily in the main compartment without flattening my clothes. It’s roomy without being ridiculous, and when you’re done using it, it folds flat, perfect for small-space storage.

This size hits that sweet spot: big enough for weekend travel, structured enough for work, and still manageable for everyday use. For women like me who juggle multiple roles (gym-goer, commuter, occasional over-packer), it’s the kind of bag that genuinely makes life easier.

## Functionality

Now, this is where the KitBrix Extra Large Tote Bag truly earns its stripes. It’s waterproof (yes, really), which means your gear stays dry even if you set it down on a damp changing room floor or get caught in a downpour. There’s also a dedicated water bottle pocket with a bright lime trim (easy to spot, even in a rush), and the zippered top keeps everything secure. Inside, compartments and mesh pockets help you stay organised, no more rummaging around for your headphones at the bottom of a black hole.

I love that it transitions seamlessly from **gym bag** to **travel tote** to **shopping companion**. It’s wipe-clean, smartly structured, and versatile enough to match your pace, whether that’s a 6 a.m. workout or a weekend city break.
