Trail Bloom deserves a standing ovation here: the size range spans an inclusive **UK 6–26**, and crucially the cut is built around real female proportions, not a shrunk-down unisex pattern. The 4-way stretch fabric flexes beautifully without losing its structure.

Built for UK conditions, the fabric is moisture-wicking yet breathable, handling damp trails and unpredictable weather nicely, and it dries quickly after wet rides. ### Genuine Female Design — 4/5 ★★★★☆
This is where Trail Bloom shines. The cut is purpose-built for women, with a relaxed shape through the thigh and a more tailored lower leg, so you get freedom without bulk. Functional touches, zipped pockets, reinforced seams, pad room and leg zips, are engineering rather than colour. It's clearly made by someone who understands what women actually need from MTB kit: no "pink it and shrink it" here.

### Transparency & User Reviews — 3/5 ★★★☆☆
