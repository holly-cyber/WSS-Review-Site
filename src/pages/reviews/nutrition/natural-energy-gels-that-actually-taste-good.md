### Nutrition Profile — 5/5 ★★★★★
This is where Purendure really shines. Each pouch gives you over **22g of carbohydrate, all from real fruit** — no maltodextrin, no glucose syrup, no artificial sweeteners — plus naturally occurring electrolytes and vitamins, so it supports hydration and recovery too. It's vegan, allergen-free and gut-friendly, and I love that I can read the ingredients and actually understand them. Clean fuel your body recognises, with nothing to decode.

### Performance — 4/5 ★★★★☆
It does the job. I've used Purendure on long runs, strength sessions and a hike where I forgot to pack lunch, and every time it kept me going without the dreaded sugar crash — a smooth, steady release rather than a spike and slump. Because it's real fruit, it digests easily: no bloating, no nausea, no regret. I also felt less wiped out afterwards, though as ever this reflects my own use rather than independently verified data.

### Value — 4/5 ★★★★☆
Natural products usually carry a premium, but Purendure feels reasonable for what you get. The **resealable pouch** means nothing goes to waste — one can stretch across a long run or several shorter workouts — which is smart spending as well as smart design. You're getting real fruit rather than synthetic fillers, and supporting a **female-founded brand**, so the value case stacks up well beyond the price per pouch.
