#### Sizing

I went for my usual size, and while it fits well, it's definitely a snug, performance cut. If you prefer a roomier fit or plan to layer up underneath, I'd recommend sizing up. The material has a little give, offering a comfortable fit without feeling restrictive, though it's not a "stretchy" fabric.

The body length in this women's cycling jacket is slightly shorter than some other women's cycling jackets, but when worn with bib shorts there was no gap at the back. However, if you're wearing shorts with a waistband, you may notice a slight gap. The silicone gripper at the hem keeps the jacket securely in place while riding.

The sleeves are close-fitting with a shaped seam at the elbow, making them feel natural in a bent elbow cycling position. Overall, this is a jacket that favours an aerodynamic fit — ideal for riders who like a streamlined silhouette.
