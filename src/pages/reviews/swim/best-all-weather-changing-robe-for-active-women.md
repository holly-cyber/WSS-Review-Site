If you want more space to change underneath, you could size up, but for me, the Medium strikes the right balance between comfort and practicality. I also appreciate the subtle design: no loud logos across the chest, just a clean, minimal aesthetic with a discreet KitBrix emblem on the back.

The **deep-cut hood** deserves a special mention too. It's big enough to fit over a chunky hat or bun and keeps the wind off your neck, something every outdoor swimmer will appreciate.
