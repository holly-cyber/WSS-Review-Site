This is a **sample news article**. It exists to demonstrate the news layout and to act as a template for pieces generated from RSS feeds in the console. It is a draft, so it is hidden on the live site — delete it whenever you like.

## How these articles are made

In the console's **News** tab you refresh the configured RSS feeds, pick one story to rewrite or several to round up, and the generator drafts an original write-up with the original stories linked as sources. You review it, tweak anything, and publish.

## Why it matters for WSS

Timely, original commentary on women's sport gives readers a reason to return between product reviews — and every article links back into the relevant review categories.
