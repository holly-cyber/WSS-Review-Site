## Overview

Ihamora is a female-founded British brand building sports bras from women-specific measurements gathered from a panel of 200+ active women. The High Impact Sports Bra targets runners, HIIT athletes, and cyclists requiring maximum support across a genuine cup range. Tested by a six-person WSS panel across B, C, D, E, and F cups over 8 weeks of run and HIIT sessions.

## WSS™ Assessment

**Pillar 1 — Inclusive Fit & Proportional Sizing: 4/5**

Available UK 8–20 across A–G cup — one of the widest size ranges tested at WSS. Sizing is accurate and consistent with UK norms. The band sizing runs slightly firm, which the brand acknowledges and advises on. Testers who normally size up in other brands found true-to-size more accurate here. The A-cup option has reduced structural engineering compared to the D+ options — a minor inconsistency but worth noting.

**Pillar 2 — Functional Performance in Use: 4/5**

Underband seams tested across 60-minute run sessions produced no chafing across any tester. The strap anchoring holds consistently at high intensity. One F-cup tester noted mild strap migration during repeated overhead movements in HIIT — this is a specific edge case and did not affect the majority of testers. Torso coverage is full and maintains position throughout activity.

**Pillar 3 — Adequate Support Standards: 5/5**

This is where the Ihamora genuinely earns its WSS™ score. High-impact support was verified across all cup sizes tested, including F cup — a cup size at which many 'high impact' bras functionally under-deliver. The encapsulation structure maintains cup independence under load. Bounce reduction measured subjectively as excellent by all panel testers at E and F cup. No tester reported inadequate support for intended use. This is a genuine Pillar 3 pass.

**Pillar 5 — Genuine Female Design: 5/5**

Ihamora is founded by women for women and the product reflects this. The pattern cutting is derived from female-specific measurements, not adapted from an existing unisex or male pattern. Colourway range is functional and wide. The founding story and design process are clearly communicated and verifiable. No pink-it-and-shrink-it concerns. Full marks.

**Pillar 7 — Transparency & User Reviews: 3/5**

Ihamora publishes testing process information and panel demographics on the website, which is above average for the category. However, quantitative testing data is not publicly available, preventing a score above 3 under WSS™ scoring rules. User reviews on the Ihamora site are broadly positive with fit and support as consistent themes. The brand is WSS-aware and actively working toward full transparency certification.

## The Verdict

One of the most credible high-impact sports bras in its price range, particularly for D cup and above where alternatives consistently underperform. The Ihamora is a clear recommendation for serious run and HIIT athletes. The only meaningful limitation is the transparency gap — verifiable testing data would take this to a 4.5+ overall.

## Buy

At £65 this is strong value for a genuinely engineered sports bra with verified cup-range performance. Available from the WSS store.
