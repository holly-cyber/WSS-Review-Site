This is a **sample blog post**. It exists to demonstrate the blog layout and to act as a template for articles generated from a theme in the console. It is a draft, so it is hidden on the live site — delete it whenever you like.

## Why open water is worth it

Moving from the pool to a lake or the sea is one of the most rewarding steps in a swimmer's journey. It also asks a bit more of your kit and your confidence.

## What the generator does

In the console's **Blog** tab you enter a content theme, and the generator writes an original article and weaves in links to the most relevant [product reviews](/reviews/) and [news pieces](/news/) automatically.

Replace this body with generated content, set `draft: false`, and the post goes live.
