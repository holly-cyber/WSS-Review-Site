## Overview

Wild & Berry produce natural sports nutrition products using whole food ingredients. The Energy Chews use dates, brown rice syrup, and natural fruit flavours as their carbohydrate base — no maltodextrin, no artificial sweeteners. Tested by four WSS panel athletes across marathon training long runs (18–28km) and back-to-back cycle sessions over six weeks.

## WSS™ Assessment

**Taste & Texture: 4/5**

Palatability mid-exercise is good. The chews are soft enough to consume without water at running pace, which not all chews achieve. The natural fruit flavour is genuine — strawberry variant in particular scored well. Some testers noted the texture becomes slightly stickier in warm conditions (18°C+), which slows consumption pace. Not a significant issue in UK conditions but worth noting for warm-weather racing.

**Nutrition Profile: 5/5**

Ingredient quality is genuinely above category average. 22g of carbohydrate per serving from natural sources, 120mg sodium, and no artificial sweeteners, colours, or preservatives. The absence of maltodextrin is notable — Wild & Berry use a brown rice syrup and date combination that provides a more sustained release profile than simple sugar alternatives. No unnecessary additives. Full marks.

**Performance: 4/5**

Energy delivery is consistent with the natural carbohydrate source — slightly slower uptake than gel alternatives but with less GI risk. Three of four testers reported no GI discomfort on long runs, which is above average for the category. One tester found the energy onset too gradual for hard intervals but noted it worked well for steady long-run pacing. At the standard application rate (one pack per 45 minutes) performance is reliable.

**Value: 4/5**

At £3.50 per serving this is mid-market for natural/premium energy chews. Cheaper than comparable clean-label alternatives from larger brands, and significantly better ingredient quality than budget options at a similar price point. Multipack pricing brings this down to approximately £3.00 per serving.

## The Verdict

Wild & Berry's Energy Chews are a strong natural fuelling option for endurance athletes prioritising ingredient quality over maximum carbohydrate density. Particularly well suited to marathon and middle-distance events where GI tolerance matters more than rapid glucose spike. The slight texture variation in warm conditions is the only meaningful practical limitation.
