## Zoggs Boronia Hi Front Swimsuit Review
### A flattering, eco-conscious suit for relaxed pool days

Finding a swimsuit that offers proper coverage without feeling frumpy — and looks elegant while it does it — is a genuine ask, especially if you want a bit more modesty for lounging and gentle swims. That's exactly the niche the [**Zoggs Boronia Hi Front Swimsuit**](https://www.zoggs.com/en_GB/boronia-hi-front-swimsuit-462383-boro#u463) fills, and it does it beautifully. For me, this is a comfortable, true-to-size suit with a lovely high neck, light support and an eco-conscious fabric story. It's not built for sprint sets, but as a stylish, sustainable everyday swimsuit, it's a delight.

## WSS™ Assessment

### Inclusive Fit & Proportional Sizing — 3/5 ★★★☆☆
The **Boronia** is refreshingly available in UK sizes **8 to 18**, and across the board I find Zoggs suits run true to size — order your usual and you'll be spot on. The **4-way stretch** fabric flexes to a real range of shapes, so it feels supportive without pinching. The one watch-out is the range itself: it stops at an 18 rather than extending into the fuller-figure sizes, so it's a solid fit story within its band rather than a truly inclusive one.

### Functional Performance in Use — 4/5 ★★★★☆
This is where the **Boronia** quietly shines. The **medium leg height and high front** give genuinely full coverage, so you feel secure whether you're swimming or sitting poolside, and the suit stays put without riding or gaping. The lined front smooths and shapes for a flattering silhouette, and the **clipback neck** is a small joy — easy to fasten when you're in a rush. Shape retention is excellent, so it holds its fit swim after swim.

### Adequate Support Standards — 3/5 ★★★☆☆
The built-in **shelf bra** offers *light* support, and that's exactly how I'd frame it: lovely for lounging, beach days and chilled swims, rather than a sprint session. There's no cup sizing or structured bust engineering here, so fuller-busted swimmers after high-impact hold will want something firmer. Within its remit — relaxed, comfortable swimming — the support is pitched just right and never restrictive.

### Thermal Regulation & Material Performance — 3/5 ★★★☆☆
The standout is the **chlorine-resistant Ecolast™ fabric**, which kept its shape, feel and colour beautifully through my testing. It's **quick-drying** and **machine washable**, which makes day-to-day care effortless. Zoggs' durability claims feel well-founded in use, though long-term wash-cycle performance isn't independently lab-verified — something to note neutrally rather than a mark against it.

### Genuine Female Design — 4/5 ★★★★☆
This is a properly women-considered suit, not a recoloured afterthought. The **high neck** is genuinely flattering, the **lined front** is engineered to smooth and shape, and the delicate Boronia print on a soft blue base is elegant without being flashy. The cut, coverage and finishing details all feel designed *for* a woman's body and how she actually wants to wear a suit — at the pool and beyond.

### Transparency & User Reviews — 4/5 ★★★★☆
Zoggs is admirably specific about what matters here: the **Ecolast™** fabric is made from recycled post-consumer plastic — around **18 x 500ml bottles per metre** — which is the kind of concrete claim I like to see. Combined with my own hands-on testing of fit, comfort and durability, there's a solid evidence base behind this suit. Independent lab data on long-term performance would round it out further.

## FAQ

### Is the Zoggs Boronia Hi Front Swimsuit true to size?
Yes — in my experience it runs true to size, so order your usual size with confidence. The 4-way stretch fabric accommodates a range of shapes without feeling tight or loose. The model pictured is 5'7" and wears a size 10.

### Does the Zoggs Boronia offer bust support?
It has a built-in shelf bra that provides light support, which suits lounging, beach days and gentle swims. It's not designed for high-impact sessions, and there's no cup sizing, so fuller-busted swimmers may want a more structured suit for intense training.

### Is the Boronia swimsuit chlorine-resistant?
Yes. It's made from Zoggs' chlorine-resistant Ecolast™ fabric, which is engineered to hold its shape, feel and colour through repeated pool use. Long-term wash-cycle performance isn't independently lab-verified, but it held up well in my testing.

### Is the Zoggs Boronia an eco-friendly swimsuit?
It is. The Ecolast™ fabric is made from recycled post-consumer plastic bottles — around 18 x 500ml bottles per metre of fabric. That makes it a strong pick if sustainability matters to you, without sacrificing comfort or durability.

### What sizes does the Zoggs Boronia Hi Front come in?
It's available in UK sizes 8 to 18, which covers most body types but stops short of a fully extended range. If you sit above an 18, you may need to look elsewhere, but within that band the fit is generous and forgiving.
