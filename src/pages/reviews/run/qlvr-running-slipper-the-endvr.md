## FAQ

### Is the QLVR ENDVR Running Slipper true to size?
No — QLVR sizing runs approximately half a size small. Most customers go up half a size from their usual trainer fit; those with wider feet go up a full size. Measure your foot length and use the size chart at qlvr.com for the most accurate guide.

### Does the WingFit™ system actually keep your foot secure without laces?
Yes — in my three months of running and gym use, the **patented WingFit™ system** (bio-based Pebax® polymer) contracts to a 360° hold the moment your foot is in. Heel lift and lateral slip were non-issues, even during dynamic lateral training.

### Can I use the QLVR ENDVR for both running and gym training?
In my experience, yes. I've used them for steady runs, cardio intervals, and strength sessions. The **Pebax® propulsion plate** and rocker profile support running efficiency, while the WingFit™ lockdown handles gym movement confidently.

### What makes QLVR women-first rather than just a women's colourway?
QLVR was founded specifically to move beyond resized men's trainers. The **ENDVR** is built on a female-specific last shaped around women's narrower heels, wider forefeet, and higher arches — a biomechanical decision, not an aesthetic one.
