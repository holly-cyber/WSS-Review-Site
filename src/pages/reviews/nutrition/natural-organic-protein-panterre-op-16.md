### Taste & Texture

<a href="https://panterre.avln.me/c/aETAtDbrwIGO" target="_blank" rel="nofollow sponsored">Panterre OP-16 Organic Protein</a> stands out for its commitment to all-natural, organic ingredients—no artificial flavourings, sweeteners, or additives. When mixed with full-fat milk, bananas, and blueberries, the taste is pleasant and not overly sweet. However, I found the cacao and cinnamon flavours quite subtle, almost undetectable to my palate, which may be a matter of personal taste. Mixing the powder with Greek yoghurt for breakfast resulted in a texture and colour that I found a little off-putting, so I'd recommend sticking to milk or smoothies for the best experience.

### Nutrition Profile

Each serving delivers an impressive 25g of protein, which is excellent for muscle recovery and daily nutrition. The product is not particularly calorific, making it suitable for those watching their energy intake. I appreciate that it's manufactured in the UK, ensuring high standards and traceability. The clean ingredient list is a major plus for me and anyone seeking a genuinely natural supplement. Since my breast cancer diagnosis, I have become especially mindful of what I eat. It is extremely important to me that any product I choose is purely natural, containing no artificial colourings or additives. My focus is on maintaining a healthy and natural diet, and I believe that avoiding artificial ingredients is a crucial part of supporting my wellbeing which is why Panterre is now part of my protein journey.
