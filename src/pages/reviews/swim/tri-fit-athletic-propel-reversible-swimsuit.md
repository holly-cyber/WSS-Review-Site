## TRI-FIT Athletic PROPEL Reversible Swimsuit Review

Finding a swimsuit that genuinely pulls its weight in the pool — without making you choose between looking great and performing well — is trickier than it sounds. That's exactly the gap the [**TRI-FIT Athletic PROPEL Reversible Swimsuit**](https://trifitathletic.avln.me/c/dtgJtDFakJfg) is aiming to fill, and from everything I've put it through, it makes a pretty compelling case for itself. Two suits in one, a seriously considered fabric choice, and strong endorsements from credible tri media: this one lands as a smart buy for the regular pool trainer, with just a few things worth knowing before you click add to basket.

## FAQ

### Is the TRI-FIT PROPEL Reversible Swimsuit suitable for triathlon racing?
It's been endorsed by **220 Triathlon** and **TRI247** as a strong training suit. For race-day use, check whether the double-layer construction suits your compression preference — single-layer race suits may offer a slight hydrodynamic advantage, but for training it performs very well.

### What fabric is the TRI-FIT PROPEL Swimsuit made from?
It's made from **Carvico Italian fabric**, a reputable performance textile known for chlorine resistance, durability, and shape retention. The exact Carvico product line isn't specified in published details — worth querying with TRI-FIT directly if fabric composition matters to you.

### Does the TRI-FIT PROPEL Swimsuit offer bust support?
Specific bust support details — such as cup sizing, underwire, or shelf bra construction — are **not published in the product specs**. The close-fit design and secure straps suggest reasonable support for low-to-medium impact swimming, but fuller-busted swimmers should seek hands-on confirmation.

### How does the reversible design work on the PROPEL Swimsuit?
The **PROPEL** features a **plain side and a patterned side**, letting you wear either as the outer layer. This effectively gives you two distinct looks from one suit. Seam comfort when reversed is not independently verified, so a test swim on both sides is a sensible first step.

### Does the TRI-FIT PROPEL Swimsuit have UV protection?
Yes — **UV protection** is listed as a feature, consistent with the Carvico fabric's known properties. A specific **UPF rating** is not published in the available specs, so if you need a confirmed UPF number for outdoor swimming, it's worth checking directly with TRI-FIT Athletic.
