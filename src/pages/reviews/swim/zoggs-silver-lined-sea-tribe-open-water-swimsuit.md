## Zoggs Sea Tribe Silver Lined Strikeback Open Water Swimsuit Review

### Thermal warmth, recycled fabric and a flattering open-water fit

If you swim outdoors like me, you'll know the eternal search for *the one*: the suit that keeps you warm, flatters your shape, and doesn't bag, sag, or drag every time you plunge into cold British water. That's exactly the gap the [**Zoggs Sea Tribe Silver Lined Strikeback**](https://www.zoggs.com/en_GB/sea-tribe-silver-lined-strikeback-swimsuit-462377-seat#u463) is built to fill. From the moment I slipped it on I knew it wasn't your average cossie — sleek, supportive and stylish — and after several weeks of pool sessions and chilly lake swims, it delivers on its core promise: keeping the heat in.

### Adequate Support Standards

<span class="wss-score"><span class="wss-score-num">3</span><span class="wss-score-stars">★★★<span class="off">☆☆</span></span></span>

I want to be clear and neutral here: **bust support is moderate**. For most swimmers and for the low-to-medium impact of steady open-water training, that's perfectly fine — the close, secure fit and **thin sports straps that stay put** in choppy water give a reasonable, reassuring hold. What it doesn't have is **built-in cups** or structured lift, so if you're a fuller-busted swimmer or simply prefer more support, you'd want a suit with internal cups or a shelf bra. A 3 reflects solid everyday support rather than any shortfall — it does what it sets out to do.

## FAQ

### Does the Zoggs Sea Tribe Silver Lined Strikeback keep you warm in cold water?
Yes — the **Thermal Silver Lining** subtly reflects body heat and genuinely takes the edge off cool-morning swims in my testing. It's not a wetsuit substitute, but for chilly lake and sea sessions it keeps the heat in noticeably better than a standard suit.

### Is the Zoggs Sea Tribe swimsuit true to size?
Zoggs sizing tends to be spot on, and this suit ran **true to size** for me. The stretch is forgiving without losing support, so if you're between sizes and prefer a snug performance fit, you can comfortably size down.

### Does the Zoggs Sea Tribe Strikeback have bust support?
Support is **moderate** — fine for most swimmers and low-to-medium impact open-water training. There are no built-in cups, so if you prefer extra lift or structured support, you may want a suit with internal cups or a shelf bra.

### What is the Zoggs Sea Tribe swimsuit made from?
It uses **Ecofeel™ fabric** made from recycled plastic bottles. It's **chlorine-resistant** and offers **UPF50+** sun protection, and in my use it held its shape and colour after repeated washing and heavy pool-and-lake swapping.

### What size range does the Zoggs Sea Tribe range go up to?
The range goes all the way up to a **size 22**, which is refreshing to see in performance swimwear. That broader span makes it a more inclusive choice than many open-water suits aimed at regular swimmers.
